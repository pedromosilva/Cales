    Breakfast (in bed?)

    If you want to have breakfast or just buy some bread to bring home, the bakeries we recommend are Broas e Broinhas, or Rainha do Pao Quente

    Ironing your clothes

    You can find the iron inside the cooking isle on the drawer blow the oven, the ironing board you can find it inside the closet in the sleeping room.

    Washing your clothes

    The wash mashine is on the level -1, you can reach it using the lift. You need a 2 Euro coin to use it. Just use the lift and it the last door at the end of the corridor. The hanger for the clothes is below the box bed.

    Hoover

    The Hoover is split in two parts. The hoover itself is below the oven on the cooking isle, and the extensions are on the hidden door on the wall next to the sleeping room entrance.

    TV

    To use the TV you just need to turn it on and choose the app MEO. Inside the app you have all the TV channels and content. If you are to use a streaming service like Netflix or Amazon Prime, please log out and log in using your own account. Don't forget to disconnect before you leave the flat. You can connect the TV to the blootooth speaker for better sound, just turn it on and use the menu on the TV to connect to it.

    Trash

    In Portugal we separate Glas, Paper and Plastic. For the normal trash you have a public black container on the street that is visible from the building entrance. To deposit the separated residuals, you walk down the street until the next crossing, there you can find the 4 types of containers to your right.

    Air conditioning

    There is only one remote for the air conditioning. You can find it on the sleeping room next to the entrance door. It works for both devices.

    Wine Cooler

    To turn on the wine cooler you just open its door and presse the power button that is inside until the display turns on.

    Remarks

    Before you leave the flat, please make sure you put away the trash, and pack back the balcony furniture as you found it. Unfornunately the sun is very strong and damages it if exposed for too long. Please bring the pillows inside the flat.
