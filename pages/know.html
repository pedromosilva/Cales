    How to reach us

    Cales apartment is 250m away from "Estação de Campanhã".
    Click on the cards below and you will be forwared to google maps to see your itinerary.

    Train, Metro or Bus

    Cales apartment is 250m away from "Estação de Campanhã". THere you can find the connections to Train, Bus and Metro

    From the airport

    To come from the Airpot you can catch the metro staying at the airport (it is the final station, so there is only one, it is the Purple Line E), you will need to change line, I recommend to do it in the station of Carolina Michaelis, you need to hop out and hop on in the same line, just switch to the line going direction "Estadio do Dragao" Line A, Blue. For This trip you need to buy a Z4 ticket.

    you have a Car

    If you would like to park your car in safety, we would recomment to park the car in the public garage underneath at Estação de Campanhã.

    To go to the city

    You can walk to the downtown. For walking we choose to start the city walk heading to Bulhao or in the evening to have dinner to the Praca dos Poveiros. There are a lot of good restaurants there.
    If you prefer go directly to Ribeira you need to buy a Z2 ticket and take the next train in Estação de Campanhã going to Porto Sao Bento. It is only one stop. As it is a final station, all the trains in Porto Sao Bento stop in Estação de Campanhã, you can take the first one driving when you get there to come back home.
