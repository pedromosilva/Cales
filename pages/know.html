    How to reach us

    Cales apartment is 250m away from "Estação de Campanhã".
    This is the best connection you have to the city in Porto. You travel directly from the airport
    Click on the cards below and you will be forwared to google maps to see your itinerary.

    by train or Bus

    Cales apartment is 250m away from "Estação de Campanhã".
    All trains and Buses, that drive to Porto stop at this station.

    by Airplane

    You can catch the only subway/metro that drives from the airport Francisco Sá Carneiro and the exit is Estação de Campanhã. Click here find the best connections.
    You need to buy a Z3 ticket "Andante". I recomend to buy a tourist pass so that you can move freely in Porto during your stay. You can find more information about the prices here.

    by Car

    If you would like to park your car in safety, we would recomment the garage at Estação de Campanhã where you can park your car for the price of x euro a day. Confirm pricing by clicking on this card.
