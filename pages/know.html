    • How to reach

    Cales apartment is 250m away from Estação de Campanhã.

    Train, Metro or Bus

    In Estação de Campanhã you can find the connections to Train, Bus and Metro(Subway).
    Please notice that the Metro station you can reach on your left around the glass building on your left.

    From the airport

    To come from the Airpot you can catch the metro staying at the airport (it is the final station, so there is only one, it is the Purple Line E), you will need to change line, I recommend to do it in the station of Carolina Michaelis. Here you just need to hop out and hop on in the same platform, just switch to the line going direction "Estadio do Dragao" Line A, color Blue. For This trip you need to buy a Z4 ticket.
    Other option it is to take an Uber or a Taxi. Please pay attention that the price should be below 20 euros. If Uber is asking for a lot more due to the high demand, considert taking a taxi instead.

    you have a Car

    If you would like to park your car in safety, we would recomment to park the car in the public garage underneath at Estação de Campanhã.

    How to go to the city

    You can walk to the downtown. For walking we choose to start the city walk heading to Bulhao or in the evening to have dinner to the Praca dos Poveiros. There are a lot of good restaurants there.
    If you prefer go directly to Ribeira you should go to the Estação de Campanhã and to buy there a Z2 ticket. You should there take the next train heading Porto Sao Bento. It is only one stop.
    Porto Sao Bento is a final station, all the trains driving from Porto Sao Bento stop in Estação de Campanhã, that means what when you are driving back home you can just take the first one departing.

    About the metro and Train Tickets

    When you buy a ticket, you always need to check the machine on the station where you are, for how many zones you are crossing along your trip.
    On the ticket machine there is a list with all the names of the stations and the respective number of zones you need to do the trip (Z2, Z3, etc.). The ticket that you buy is rechargeable.
    You can reload it and use it multiple times. After you purchase your ticket on the machine, you need to validate it in the yellow machines that are on the platform. The machines will beep and tell you how many trips you have left on the same card.
    A Z2 ticket is normally enough for any trip inside the downtonw of Porto. If you leave the downtown you will probably need to change to a different Z number. For a complete manual in German language click here.
