    Porto

    Day 1, Wine with a view

    This route will take you to the heart of Porto.

    As you probably know, the city of Porto is known for Port wine. Port wine was produced in the Douro Valley and brought using Rebelo boats to Porto, where it would be traded and exported. In Vila Nova de Gaia, across the river, you can still see the warehouses, which we call caves and find a lot of museums that portrait this history and can tell you this history way better than we do.

    What we can tell you is the best spots that you can go to, if you just want to chill on a lounge with an amazing view drinking a Cocktail with Port, know the heart of the city and be amazed by the Portuguese hospitality.

    Enjoy!

    First you leave the flat and go down the street to "Estacao de Campanha". In this train station, you can take any train that drives to "Porto S. Bento". It is only 1 station away.

    Once you arrive to Estacao de Porto S. Bento, turn left and walk to "Sé do Porto". This is your first stop. Besides the amazing church and monastery, you can appreciate the view over the places you are about to visit.

    Walk to the left side around the church and head to "Ponte D. Luís". From the top tray you have a wonderful view now from both sides of the river.

    Once you cross the bridge, you will come to "Serra do Pilar" on you left side, and "Jardim do Morro" on your right side. From these sightseing points you have a great view over the old town.

    The next step is to go down to "Cais de Gaia". You can choose to walk down to the lower tray of the bridge and start from there, or to take the "Teleférico".

    Cais de Gaia

    In Cais de Gaia you have now the opportunity to do a Port wine tasting. Just choose the place exhibition it works best for you. We like to visit Porto Cálem. If you don't wish to do it now. You also have the chance to do or book a boat tour.
    If you prefer to walk a bit more, you can start going up the hill to Taylors. If you didn't do a visit/tasting, we recomend you to do it here.

    From Taylors, you might be thinking that it is time for a coffee, a Cocktail or even a Luxurious Lunch. So go and head to the Yeatmann hotel.

    This hotel has the best view in the city. You won't regret to go there.
